Dance

I like choreographing dance that combines traditional and modern elements together. When choreographing elements of Han and Tang Dynasty cultures into modern sign language dancing, I was excited that the past and the present bonded together in the dancers’ movements.

Guzheng

I have played Guzheng -- a Chinese piano with over 2500 years of history -- sicne elementry school. I enjoy playing it because when gently plucking the strings of the guzheng, I imagined that the strong emotion expressed in each note linked my soul with the ancient composer’s.
